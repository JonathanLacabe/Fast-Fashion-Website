Welcome to Paldi!

The best clothing in central north-western India


Welcome to Paldi Garment Industrial Complex! We are a small textiles factory in Jaipur utilizing a variety of fabrics and materials and specializing in Shirts, Caps, Coats, and Jackets.
We are a family-owned business that serves our community with the highest-quality garments in the region. Our clothing is completely environmentally sustainable and we are obtain our animal-based raw fabrics from humane and cruelty-free farms.
In order to make purchases, go to the 'Shop' section of the website and select the products you want to buy and decide in the 'Cart' section how many of the product you would like to purchase.
